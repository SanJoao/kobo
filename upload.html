How to Find Your Highlights File

Follow these steps to locate your Kobo database file:

  1. Connect your Kobo eReader to your computer.
  2. Open the Kobo drive. You should see a hidden folder named .kobo (you may need to enable "Show hidden files" on your computer).
  3. Inside the .kobo folder, find the file named KoboReader.sqlite.

This file contains all your highlights and notes.
